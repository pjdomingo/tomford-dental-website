What We Offer

Services Offered

Comprehensive dental care — from preventive checkups to cosmetic and surgical treatments.

Consultation

Initial assessment of your oral health to plan personalized, effective treatment.

TMJ Consultation

Specialized evaluation and treatment planning for temporomandibular joint disorders.

Periapical X-ray

Detailed radiographic imaging to detect root issues, bone loss, and hidden decay.

Oral Prophylaxis

Professional cleaning to remove plaque and tartar and maintain optimal gum health.

Deep Scaling

Thorough sub-gingival cleaning to treat gum disease and prevent further periodontal damage.

Fluoride Treatment

Protective fluoride application to strengthen enamel and prevent cavities in all ages.

Tooth Filling

Durable composite restorations that repair cavities and blend seamlessly with natural teeth.

Root Canal Treatment

Careful removal of infected pulp tissue to save the tooth and relieve severe pain.

Pit & Fissure Sealant

Protective coating applied to back teeth to prevent decay in deep grooves and fissures.

Esthetic Restoration

Natural-looking repairs for chipped, cracked, or discolored teeth using advanced composites.

Jacket Crown

Full-coverage porcelain cap that restores strength, shape, and appearance of a damaged tooth.

Teeth Whitening

Safe, professional bleaching treatments that lift deep stains and brighten your smile.

Veneers

Ultra-thin porcelain shells bonded to the front of teeth for a flawless, magazine-ready smile.

Dentures

Custom-made removable prosthetics that restore your full smile, bite function, and facial structure.

Fixed Bridge

Permanently anchored tooth replacement that bridges the gap left by missing teeth.

Orthodontic Treatment

Traditional and modern braces to straighten teeth and correct bite alignment for lasting results.

Retainers

Custom retainers to hold your teeth in their corrected position after orthodontic treatment.

Dental Splint

A custom oral appliance for stabilizing teeth affected by trauma, bruxism, or TMJ disorders.

Night Guard

Protective custom guard worn during sleep to prevent grinding damage and morning jaw pain.

Tooth Extraction

Careful, gentle removal of severely damaged or decayed teeth to protect overall oral health.

Wisdom Tooth Extraction

Surgical removal of impacted or problematic wisdom teeth to prevent pain, crowding, and infection.
