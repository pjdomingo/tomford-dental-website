2 How a booking flows

From the moment a patient clicks "Book a Consultation" to the final confirmation, here's exactly what happens behind the scenes.

Patient side — when they book

1
Patient visits tomforddental.com and clicks Book a Consultation.
2
They pick a service (e.g. Veneers, Cleaning), an available date, and an open time slot.
3
They fill in their name, email, and phone, then submit.
4
They immediately receive a "Request Received" email — this is NOT a confirmation yet, just a heads-up that you'll review.

Clinic side — when you receive a booking

1
You get a "🔔 New Request" email at the clinic inbox with all the patient details and a button to review.
2
A new row appears in the Bookings Sheet with status Pending.
3
You log into the Admin Panel and see it in the Pending Requests tab.
4
You decide: Approve ✅ or Decline ❌.

What happens after you approve

  • The patient gets a "Confirmed!" email with the appointment details and an "Add to Google Calendar" button.
  • The appointment is added to the clinic's Google Calendar automatically.
  • The booking row in the Sheet flips to Approved.
  • That time slot is locked — no other patient can book it.

What happens if you decline

  • You can optionally write a short note explaining why (e.g. "Dr. Tom unavailable that day").
  • The patient gets a polite "Update on Your Booking Request" email with your note.
  • The slot is freed up — another patient can book it.
  • The booking row stays in the Sheet with status Declined for your records.

3 How to test it

The best way to learn is to do a full test booking yourself, end-to-end. This takes about 5 minutes.

Step 1 — Make a fake booking

  1. Open tomforddental.com in your browser.
  2. Click Book a Consultation.
  3. Use a real email address you can check (e.g. your personal Gmail) so you can see the patient-side emails.
  4. Pick any service, date, and time. Submit.

Step 2 — Check the four places

  1. Your inbox — you should see the "Request Received" email within ~30 seconds.
  2. The clinic inbox — you should see the "🔔 New Request" notification.
  3. The Bookings Sheet — open it and confirm a new row was added at the bottom with status "Pending".
  4. The Admin Panel — log in and confirm the booking shows up under "Pending Requests".

Step 3 — Approve it

  1. In the Admin Panel, click Approve on your test booking.
  2. Check your patient inbox — you should get the "Confirmed!" email with the calendar button.
  3. Check Google Calendar — you should see the new event for that date and time.
  4. Back in the Sheet — confirm the status changed to "Approved".

Step 4 — Try declining (optional, on a different test booking)

  1. Make another fake booking.
  2. In the Admin Panel, click Decline.
  3. Type a short reason like "Just testing — please ignore."
  4. Confirm the patient gets the rejection email and the Sheet row says "Declined".
✅ If all of the above worked, the system is healthy and ready for real patients. If anything didn't work, write down which step failed and message Paolo with the details.

4 Admin Panel tour

The Admin Panel is where you'll spend most of your time. Here's what each section does.

📥 Pending Requests

New bookings that haven't been reviewed yet. You'll see the patient's name, contact info, the service they want, and the date/time. Approve or decline from here. The number badge in the sidebar shows you how many are waiting.

📅 Calendar View

A monthly overview of all your appointments — both pending and approved. Click any event to jump to it, or click the "Open Google Calendar" button to view it in your full Google Calendar. (New)

📋 All Bookings

The full history — pending, approved, declined. Useful if a patient calls and you need to look up their appointment.

🔢 Stats at the top

Quick counts: how many total bookings, how many pending, how many approved, how many declined.

5 Bookings Sheet tour

The Google Sheet is the source of truth. Everything the booking system uses (your settings, services, and bookings) lives here.

Tab: Services

One service per row in column A. To add or remove a service from the booking form, edit this tab — no code changes needed.

Tab: Config

Settings like opening hours, slot length, clinic phone, etc. Edit the values in column B. Changes show up on the website within a minute. Don't change column A keys unless asked — those are the variable names the code looks for.

Common things you might want to change:

  • OPEN_TIME / CLOSE_TIME — daily opening hours
  • SLOT_DURATION_MINS — how long each appointment slot is (currently 60 minutes)
  • OPEN_DAYS — which days you're open (1=Mon, 6=Sat)
  • ADVANCE_BOOKING_DAYS — how far ahead patients can book (currently 30 days)
  • CLINIC_PHONE, CLINIC_EMAIL, CLINIC_HOURS — info shown in the email footers

Tab: Appointments

This is the list of every booking. Don't manually edit this — let the Admin Panel manage it. You can use it to read or filter, but writing changes here directly can break things.

6 Common questions

What if a patient picks a slot that's already taken?

They can't — taken slots are automatically hidden from the booking form. The system also double-checks at submission time, so even if someone tries to book the exact same slot at the same moment as another patient, only the first one goes through.

Can patients book on Sundays?

By default, no — the clinic is closed on Sundays per the OPEN_DAYS setting. To change that, edit the Config tab.

Can patients book at 3am or right at closing time?

No. There's a 1-hour buffer (you can change this with SLOT_BUFFER_MINS), so patients can only book slots starting at least 1 hour from now. Slots are also limited to within your opening hours.

What if I miss a request and forget to approve it?

The patient won't get a confirmation, but the slot is still held for them. Best practice: check the Pending tab once or twice a day. If you decide later it won't work, decline and the patient gets notified.

Can I edit a booking after approving it?

Not yet — that feature is coming soon. For now, if a patient needs to reschedule, decline the original booking with a friendly note asking them to rebook for a new slot.

What if I approve by mistake?

The calendar event will be created and the confirmation email sent. The fastest fix is to call the patient directly to explain. Then in Google Calendar, delete the event manually. (We'll add an "undo" feature in a future update.)

How do I add a new service?

Open the Sheet → Services tab → type the new service name in column A on a new row. That's it — it shows up in the booking form within a minute.

How do I change clinic opening hours?

Open the Sheet → Config tab → edit OPEN_TIME (e.g. 09:00) and CLOSE_TIME (e.g. 19:00). Use 24-hour format. Make sure the cells are formatted as plain text (not as time values).

Can I see who declined a booking and why?

Yes — go to the All Bookings tab in the Admin Panel. Declined bookings stay in the list with their status. The reason you typed is saved in the Sheet's Notes column.

What if the website goes down or stops accepting bookings?

Message Paolo immediately. In the meantime, patients can still call or email the clinic. The website is hosted on Cloudflare and the booking backend on Google's infrastructure — both are very reliable, but if anything is wrong we'll fix it within hours.
